*Tips for using these templates:*

Always personalize the studio name, city, and any specific details you know about them before sending. The templates are starting points, not scripts. If you've visited the studio, been to a class, or have any genuine connection to mention — put it in. That one sentence of real context does more work than the rest of the email combined.

# DIY Studio Search — Project Instructions

## What This Is

DIY Studio Search is a directory of DIY studios and craft classes, built to scale across all 50 states. The business model is freemium: free listings for all studios, paid featured placement for studios that want top-of-results visibility and a richer listing card. The operator is John — solo builder, part-time. This is a side project running alongside a full-time banking career.

The live file for the directory is `index.html`. Everything in this folder is part of that project. The site now has multi-page navigation (Find Studios / Submit a Studio), craft and city filters, tag-based filtering, and a featured card tier with pull quote and booking button.

## Guiding Principles

**List first, outreach second.** Never generate outreach content or suggest contacting a studio that isn't already in the directory. The entire credibility of the pitch depends on the studio already being findable on the map.

**Free layer first, paid conversion later.** Don't push monetization content until Phase 3 (Day 90+). The current priority is listing density and organic traffic.

**Scarcity is a feature.** Featured listings are one slot per city per craft category. When writing about the product or pitch, frame scarcity as a deliberate product decision, not a limitation.

**ROI framing over feature lists.** John has a banking and financial crime investigation background. When drafting pitch content, pricing rationale, or conversion emails, lean on cost-per-acquisition and attribution language. Studio owners have rarely had anyone apply financial ROI framing to their marketing spend — that's a genuine differentiator.

**Tone is peer-to-peer.** John is a crafter himself (intermediate potter, beginner leatherworker). All outreach, social copy, and listing descriptions should sound like they're from a real person in the craft community, not a directory product company.

## Monetization Structure

- Free listing: basic card, searchable by city and craft type
- Featured listing: top placement, photo, pull quote, Book a Class button, monthly click report, Featured badge
- Pricing: $25/month standard, $20/month early adopter (first 90 days)
- One featured slot per city per craft category — enforce this in all product copy

---

## Geographic Scope

Currently live in North Carolina. Expansion to all 50 states is the long-term goal — Phase 4+. When generating listings, copy, or SEO content, keep geographic targeting tight to the states currently live. Do not generate listings for states not yet in the directory.

---

## What to Avoid

- Don't add studios to outreach drafts that aren't confirmed to be in the directory
- Don't soften the scarcity pitch ("limited slots available" is correct, not a caveat)
- Don't write in a corporate or startup marketing tone — keep it human and craft-community-adjacent
- Don't suggest paid ad spend as a growth channel; the strategy is organic SEO and Instagram
- Don't confuse the project phases — check `launch_plan.md` if unsure where in the timeline something belongs
